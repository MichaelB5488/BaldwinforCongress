Meet Michael Baldwin

Michael Baldwin is a dedicated father, community advocate, and everyday working American running for the U.S. House of Representatives. He believes government should serve families, protect freedoms, and restore honesty to Washington. Michael is committed to fighting for affordable living, strong local economies, and safe communities.

Key Issues

  • Lowering the cost of living for working families
  • Protecting parental rights and family values
  • Small business support and job creation
  • Government transparency and accountability
  • Public safety and law enforcement support
