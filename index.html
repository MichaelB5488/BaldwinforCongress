Meet Michael

Michael Baldwin is running for Congress to bring common sense, transparency, and real representation back to New Jersey’s 10th District. As a husband and father of two — an energetic one‑year‑old and a curious eight‑year‑old — Michael knows what working families are facing every day.

He’s not backed by political machines or corporate donors — he’s backed by real people who are tired of being ignored. Michael’s mission is simple: fight for practical solutions, accountability, and a Congress that actually works for the people it serves.

Why Michael is Running

  • To give voters a real independent choice — not the same recycled party politics.
  • To focus on safer communities, economic opportunity, and affordable living.
  • To bring transparency, fairness, and honesty to Washington.
