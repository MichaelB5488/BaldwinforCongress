Meet Michael Baldwin

Michael Baldwin is a dedicated father, community advocate, and everyday working American running for the U.S. House of Representatives. He believes government should serve families, protect freedoms, and restore honesty to Washington.

Michael is committed to fighting for affordable living, strong local economies, and safe communities across New Jersey’s 10th District.

Issues & Priorities

Lower Costs for Families

Cut the cost of groceries, rent, gas, and everyday essentials by focusing on practical, pro‑worker solutions.

Public Safety & Strong Communities

Support law enforcement, stop violent crime, and fund community programs that keep our neighborhoods safe.

Jobs & Small Business

Back small businesses, create good local jobs, and keep opportunity here at home.

Parental Rights & Education

Parents deserve a real voice in their kids’ education and a school system focused on success.

Transparency & Accountability

End backroom deals, demand clear answers, and put voters — not special interests — first.

Healthcare That Works

Affordable, accessible care and real support for mental health and addiction recovery.
